Sustainable Oceans

Information

99% of the living space on the planet by volume is oceans and over 3 billion people depend on ocean biodiversity for their livelihood

Goal

Sustainable Development Goal 14 is about sustainability and conservation of the oceans and to prevent and stop ocean pollution. Ocean pollution is a huge issue, as it affects the many people around the world.

Why it is a Problem

5% of all global GDP is coastal and marine resources. Oceans absorb 30% of all carbon dioxide from Human global warming. The acidity of the ocean raised by 26% since the start of the Industrial Revolution and coastal waters are deteriorating due to pollution and eutrophication.

How it Affects Us

High school students will not be able to eat fish or go to the beach if this continues. Ocean pollution affects everyone throughout the world. If kids want to be marine biologists, then they will eventually not have a job.

How to Help

Picking up trash on beaches or participating in beach cleanups make a difference. Recycling and disposing of waste propely helps too. Donating to organizations that help solve this problem is another option. click here to donate to The Ocean Cleanup
